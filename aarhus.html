The Library Field

libraryfield.org | metro.org | natehill.net

Library Field is an artistic research project that proposes building a different kind of institution, one that is focused on processes rather than artifacts. For a Library Field, we would dedicate a parcel of land to an experimental new vision for what a sustainable institution could be if it is of, by, and for the land it inhabits. Libraries serve as an interdisciplinary crossroads for governments, schools, businesses, communities, or any other group that wishes to pool and share knowledge. The Library “Field” is a playful take on that interdisciplinary nature, and invites participants to engage in the production of a different kind of institution.
